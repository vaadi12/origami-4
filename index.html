1. CAMEL


FACTS YOU SHOULD KNOW:-

  1. Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months!!
  2. When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 182 liters in one go.
  3. Camels are very strong and can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day.

3. PIGEON

FACTS YOU SHOULD KNOW:-

  1. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities. They can detect sounds at far lower frequencies than humans are able to, and can thus hear distant storms and volcanoes.
  2. Pigeons and humans have lived in close proximity for thousands of years. The first recordings of this date back to Mesopotamis, modern Iraq, in 3000bc.
  3. Although pigeon droppings are seen by some as a problem in modern society, a few centuries ago pigeon guano was seen as extremely valuable.

5. RABBIT

FACTS YOU SHOULD KNOW:-

  1. There are more than 30 species around the world, and while they live in many different environments, they have many things in common.
  2. Rabbits and hares are in the same taxonomic family, Leporidae, but they are in different genera.
  3. Rabbits are very social creatures and live in large groups called colonies. The busiest time of day for rabbits is at dusk and dawn.
